about=This plugin integrates the TerraMind Geospatial Foundation Model (GFM) to automate the detection of floods and burn scars. While Foundation Models offer high accuracy in natural hazard analysis, they are often inaccessible to practitioners who rely on desktop GIS software rather than Python-based environments. Using the terratorch framework, this plugin enables tiled inference on Sentinel-2 and Sentinel-1 imagery directly within the QGIS interface. By leveraging TerraMind’s multi-modal architecture, the tool provides a streamlined workflow for disaster mapping, bridging the gap between large-scale research models and practical, open-source geospatial applications.
